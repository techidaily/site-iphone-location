With GPS, you can track the live locations of your destination in seconds. In fact, you can also track any human depending on their live location. A majority of people forget to turn on GPS services on their respective Apple iPhone 14s. Thus, most times, users don't know how to turn on GPS back. Henceforth, GPS not working issues become evident. To enable GPS service on Apple iPhone 14, follow these steps:  

- ****Step 1:**** Launch your Apple iPhone 14 Settings, scroll down, and find “Privacy & Security” from the menu.

### Way 2: Simple Restart Apple iPhone 14

Restarting an Apple iPhone 14 is the ultimate way to rectify underlying problems. Sometimes GPS is not working because of cache or network problems, and restarting Apple iPhone 14 can help. For restarting an Apple iPhone 14, these steps will help you:

- ****Step 1:**** From the Apple iPhone 14 settings, move into the "General" category, scroll down, and press "Shut Down." Now slide right to power off your Apple iPhone 14.

- ****Step 2:**** Wait for some time till your device gets powered off. After this, hold the “Power” button until the Apple logo appears to restart your device.

### Way 3: Force Restart Apple iPhone 14

Sometimes your Apple iPhone 14 may get hung up due to an underlying issue. So, when your device is not responding, you cannot restart it from settings. In this scenario, you need to force restart Apple iPhone 14. To do this, perform these simple instructions:

- ****Apple iPhone 14 6, 6S Plus, and Older:**** Simultaneously press and hold the “Home” and “Sleep/Wake” buttons. Make sure to keep holding both buttons until you see the Apple logo. Finally, release the buttons when the logo appears.
- ****Apple iPhone 14 7 or 7 Plus:**** Start by holding the “Sleep/Wake” button and “Volume Down” button at the same time. Afterward, stop holding the buttons and release them when you see the Apple logo.
- ****Apple iPhone 14 8 and Later:**** Initially, quickly press and release the “Volume Up” button, then perform the same with the “Volume Down” button. After this, press and hold the “Sleep/Wake” button, and release the button once the Apple logo appears.
