iPhone returning with the error Location Not Available involves issues caused by the Apple iPhone XS Max devices GPS. Location Not Available in the Find My app typically means that the Apple iPhone XS Max device you are trying to locate is either turned off, not connected to the internet, or has disabled location services.

Similarly, if the Apple iPhone XS Max device has disabled location services, it will not be able to provide its location to the app. Its important to note that this message does not necessarily mean that the Apple iPhone XS Max device is lost or stolen, but rather that it is unable to be tracked at that moment.

The Location Not Available message can appear on the Find My app for various reasons. Here are some possible explanations:

One common reason for this error is that the Apple iPhone XS Max device you are trying to locate has its location services turned off. Location services are essential for apps like Find My to accurately determine the Apple iPhone XS Max devices location. To resolve this issue, the Apple iPhone XS Max device owner needs to enable location services in their device settings.

  • 2. Poor or No Internet Connection

The Find My app relies on a stable internet connection to transmit location data. Encourage the Apple iPhone XS Max device owner to check their internet connection and try again later.   • 3. Incorrect Date and Time Settings

The Date & Time Settings of the Apple iPhone XS Max are not set properly. You may have tried to manually set the date, time, and the appropriate zone that the Apple iPhone XS Max has to follow. Technical anomalies across the settings will cause issues with the Apple iPhone XS Max devices location settings.

  • 4. Privacy Settings

Apple prioritizes user privacy, and as a result, users have control over sharing their location information. If the person you are trying to locate has set their privacy settings to restrict location sharing, you will receive a Location Not Available message. In this case, the Apple iPhone XS Max device owner needs to adjust their privacy settings to allow location sharing with the Find My app.

  • 5. Device Turned Off or Battery Depleted

If the Apple iPhone XS Max device you are tracking is turned off or its battery has completely drained, the Find My app will not be able to provide the location information. This is a common occurrence when trying to locate a lost or stolen device. In such cases, you may need to wait until the Apple iPhone XS Max device is turned on or charged to retrieve its location.

Occasionally, technical glitches or temporary server issues can cause the Location Not Available error, which will require a restart of the Apple iPhone XS Max device. In conclusion, the Location Not Available error in the Find My app can be caused by a variety of factors, including disabled location services, poor internet connection, privacy settings, device status, or technical glitches.
